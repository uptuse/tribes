TROUBLESHOOTING

  • No mic for voice chat: click the camera/mic icon in the address bar to grant permission, then refresh.
  • Low FPS: open Options → Video and drop Graphics Quality to Medium or Low.
  • Lag / rubber-banding: check the connection-quality bar in the top-right; below 2 bars indicates a network issue. Try the Casual queue if Ranked has long routing.
  • Crashes / freezes: reload the page; your position will resume mid-match if you reconnect within 30s.
  • Voice peer too loud: open the scoreboard (Tab) and click the 🔇 next to their name to mute that player only.

REPORT A BUG

Open a GitHub issue at github.com/uptuse/tribes/issues with:

  • Browser + OS version
  • Steps to reproduce
  • Expected vs actual behaviour
  • Console output (DevTools → Console)
